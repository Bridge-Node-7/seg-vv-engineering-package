## Correct Terms

## Evidence Boundary

The repository is a framework. It contains no completed experimental proof. Treat source statements as source-reported claims unless a completed evidence package exists.

## Allowed Work

- Improve clarity and consistency.
- Improve controls and safety language.
- Improve templates and evidence traceability.
- Review completed run folders.
- Summarize evidence without overclaiming.
- Preserve null results.

## Not Allowed

- Do not claim the SEG is proven.
- Do not claim over-unity, antigravity, weight loss, flight, cooling, or propulsion without protocol-matched raw data and reviewer signoff.
- Do not add personal contact details, private profiles, private messages, or unnecessary biographical material.
- Do not add high-voltage construction instructions, discharge schematics, or DIY lethal-energy procedures.
- Do not update claim status from narrative alone.

## Claim Update Rule

A claim may move upward only when the run folder includes:

- completed trial log,
- raw data,
- field maps,
- video or motion evidence,
- instrument notes,
- evidence manifest,
- SHA-256 hashes,
- reviewer signoff.

## Style Rule

Be clear, concise, source-bounded, and safety-bounded. State uncertainty plainly. No hype.

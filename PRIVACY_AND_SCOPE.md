# Privacy and Scope

This repository is designed for public release and disciplined technical review.

## Included

- Source-level identification needed for traceability: public video reference, speaker/source subject name, and claim categories.
- V&V methods, protocols, templates, safety boundaries, and evidence tracking.

## Not Included

- Personal contact information.
- Private profiles or private communications.
- Raw private transcripts unless separately approved for release.
- Personal biographical details not needed for V&V.
- High-voltage construction guides or discharge schematics.
- Claims that exceed the available evidence.

## Public Safety Boundary

The repository may document that high voltage is hazardous. It must not teach unqualified users how to build or operate high-voltage discharge systems.
